About Eunice

Hello and welcome! I'm Eunice – a passionate tailor, proud mother, and entrepreneur. My journey in the world of fashion began in the late '90s when I enrolled in a tailoring course at college. Since then, I've been creating beautiful pieces and running a boutique in Nairobi, Kenya.

Over the years, my expertise in fashion design, especially in crafting stunning "Kitenge" outfits, has earned me the opportunity to work with over 500 satisfied clients, both locally and nationally. I've also had the privilege of training young women in the art of designing and creating their own "Kitenge" clothing.

More recently, I've expanded my reach to international clients, which inspired me to launch an online platform to showcase my work and bring my unique creations to a global audience. Join me on this exciting journey as we celebrate individuality through stylish, one-of-a-kind "Kitenge" outfits.

~ Stay Styled by Eunice
